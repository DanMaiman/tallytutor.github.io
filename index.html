The problem? It'll blow your mind.

In 2008, there was a 77% graduation rate from high school, below that of most developed countries including Canada and the Czech Republic.

Our Solution: Provide accesible tutors to the local Tallahassee community.

A study conducted back in the early eighties by Peter Cohen, James Kulik, and Chen-Lin Kulik revealed that effects of tutoring on both tutors and students were positive in the areas of learning, attitude toward subject matter, and self-concept. Win-win anyone?
